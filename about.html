About us

Chai Lo Ji – More Than Just Chai

A Journey of Flavor, Tradition & Togetherness

At Chai Lo Ji, chai isn’t just a drink—it’s a celebration of life’s simple joys. Born from a passion for authentic flavors, our journey began with a vision to bring people together over a cup of chai.

We’re proud to offer a menu that blends traditional recipes with contemporary twists, ensuring there’s something for everyone. From your morning pick-me-up to evening chai dates, Chai Lo Ji is here to make every moment special.

Become a Part of the Chai Lo Ji Family

Dreaming of running your own chai café? Join the Chai Lo Ji family and share the joy of chai with your community. We provide all the support and guidance you need to build your own successful chai café.
